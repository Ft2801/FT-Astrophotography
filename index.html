TStar

TStar stands out as a powerful image processing platform based on C++17 and Qt6 architecture, explicitly designed to meet the needs of modern astrophotography and produce scientific-grade results.

The software provides a flexible environment for managing linear raw data, offering total control through advanced algorithms for noise reduction, star removal, and detail enhancement. With native AI integration and stellar calibration tools, it represents the absolute top for scientifically solid editing.
